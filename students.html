Our Students

Students from many states such as Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Delhi and Uttar Pradesh are pursuing studies in the schools of the institution.

Student Life

Students at Veda Vijnaana Vishtaram follow a traditional Gurukula system, living on campus and dedicating themselves fully to Vedic studies under the guidance of experienced teachers.

Facilities

  • Residential accommodation
  • Nutritious vegetarian meals (Anna Danam)
  • Study materials and books
  • Traditional learning environment
  • Sports and recreational facilities
  • Medical care

Student Achievements

Our students have excelled in various Vedic competitions and examinations, earning recognition for their mastery of Vedic recitation and knowledge.

Alumni

Graduates of Veda Vijnaana Vishtaram serve as teachers, priests, and scholars, carrying forward the tradition of Vedic learning across India and abroad.
