Temple Puja & Archana अर्चना विद्यालयः

Temple Puja and Archana training provides comprehensive knowledge of deity worship, temple rituals, and ceremonial procedures according to Agama traditions.

Course Overview

This program trains students in the art and science of temple worship, deity installation, daily rituals, and festival celebrations according to traditional Agama Shastras.

Curriculum

  • Agama Shastra Fundamentals
  • Pratishtha Vidhi (Deity Installation)
  • Nitya Puja (Daily Worship)
  • Shodashopacharas (16 Services)
  • Abhisheka Vidhanam
  • Alankara (Decoration)
  • Naivedya (Food Offerings)
  • Utsava Vidhis (Festival Rituals)

Practical Training

  • Mantra Recitation for Worship
  • Mudras and Nyasas
  • Kalasha Puja
  • Homa Procedures
  • Temple Management
