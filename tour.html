Veda Vijnaana Vishtaram is a premier hub for Veda, Vedangas and Veda Bhashya in the Royal City of Mysore in Southern India. Located on the slopes of Sacred Chamundi Hills (4th among the 18 Shakti Peethas), provides free and quality education along with essentials such as food, clothes and basic medical care to about 70 students.
