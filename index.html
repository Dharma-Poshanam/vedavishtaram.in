Academic Associations & Affiliations

Veda Vijnaana Vishtaram is proud to be associated with premier institutions dedicated to Sanskrit and Vedic studies:

A premier institution dedicated to the study and preservation of Vedic knowledge and traditions in Karnataka.

Karnataka Sanskrit University

Esteemed state university promoting Sanskrit education and research, fostering traditional scholarship.

Leading central university dedicated to the promotion and development of Sanskrit language and literature across India.
