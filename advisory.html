Advisory Committee

The Advisory Committee provides guidance and oversight to ensure the institution maintains the highest standards of Vedic education and traditional values.

Role and Responsibilities

  • Provide strategic direction for the institution
  • Ensure adherence to traditional Vedic teaching methods
  • Review and approve curriculum changes
  • Guide faculty development programs
  • Support fundraising and resource development
  • Maintain quality standards in education

Committee Members

The Advisory Committee comprises distinguished scholars, traditional Vedic experts, and community leaders dedicated to preserving and propagating Vedic knowledge.
