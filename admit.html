Admissions

Regular Admissions in the school are very limited due to availability constraints.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 8-15 years for Veda schools
  • Basic knowledge of Sanskrit (preferred)
  • Commitment to residential Gurukula system
  • Parental consent and support
  • Good health and physical fitness

Application Process

  1. Send expression of interest via email
  2. Submit application form with required documents
  3. Personal interview with faculty
  4. Admission decision communicated within 2 weeks

Required Documents

  • Birth certificate
  • Recent photographs
  • Academic records (if applicable)
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Parent/Guardian identification

Fee Structure

Education at Veda Vijnaana Vishtaram is provided free of cost, including boarding, lodging, and study materials. The institution is supported by donations from generous patrons.
