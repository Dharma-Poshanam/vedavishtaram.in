Shrouta School श्रौत विद्यालयः

Shrouta refers to the elaborate Vedic rituals and sacrifices prescribed in the Shrouta Sutras, performed with multiple fires and priests.

Course Overview

The Shrouta school trains students in the theory and practice of major Vedic sacrifices and rituals according to Shrouta Sutra traditions.

Curriculum

  • Shrouta Sutras (Aashwalayana, Aapastamba, etc.)
  • Agni Pratishthapanam (Fire Installation)
  • Soma Yagnas
  • Darsha Pournamasa (New Moon & Full Moon Sacrifices)
  • Chaturmasya (Seasonal Sacrifices)
  • Agnihotra (Daily Fire Ritual)
  • Agnishtoma and other Soma Sacrifices

Ritual Categories

  • Haviryajnas (Oblation Sacrifices)
  • Somayajnas (Soma Sacrifices)
  • Pashuyajnas (Animal Sacrifices)
  • Sattras (Extended Rituals)
