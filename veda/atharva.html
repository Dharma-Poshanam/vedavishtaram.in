Course Structure

  • Atharva Veda Shounaka/ Paippalaada Samhita - 20 Kaandas
  • Gopatha Brahmanam - 11 Prapaathaka (Divided into 3 Parts)
  • Upanishat - 12 Prapaathaka
  • Pada Paatha, Krama Paatha - Each 20 Kaanda (Recently Some scholars started this practice)

Vedanga

  • 1) Shikshaa: Atharva Praatishaakhyam; Chaturadhyayikaa, Maanduki Shiksha
  • 2) Vyakaranam: Panineeyam
  • 3) Chhandas: Pingalam
  • 4) Niruktam: Yaaskam
  • 5) Jyotishyam: Lagadham
  • 6) Kalpa: Maanava Shrouta Sutram, Koushika Gruhya Sutram, Atharva Parishishtam
