Smaarta Prayoga School स्मार्त प्रयोग विद्यालयः

Smaarta Prayoga refers to the ritualistic practices followed by householders according to Smriti texts and Grihya Sutras.

Course Overview

The Smaarta Prayoga school trains students in the practical application of Vedic rituals for daily worship, life-cycle ceremonies, and household observances according to traditional Smriti guidelines.

Curriculum

  • Nitya Karmas (Daily Rituals)
  • Sandhya Vandanam
  • Grihya Prayogas (Household Ceremonies)
  • Shodasha Samskaras (16 Life-Cycle Rituals)
  • Vrata and Utsava Vidhis
  • Puja Vidhanam
