Jyotisha School ज्योतिष विद्यालयः

Jyotisha is one of the six Vedangas, the science of astronomy and astrology as described in Vedic texts.

Course Overview

The Jyotisha school provides comprehensive training in Vedic astronomy, calendar systems, and astrological sciences based on classical texts.

Curriculum

  • Vedanga Jyotisha - Lagadha
  • Siddhanta Jyotisha (Astronomical Calculations)
  • Samhita Jyotisha (Mundane Astrology)
  • Hora Shastra (Predictive Astrology)
  • Panchanga Ganita (Calendar Calculations)
  • Muhurta Shastra (Electional Astrology)
  • Prasna Shastra (Horary Astrology)

Classical Texts

  • Brihat Samhita - Varahamihira
  • Brihat Jataka - Varahamihira
  • Surya Siddhanta
  • Jataka Parijata
  • Hora Shastra - Parashara
