Rigveda School ऋग्वेद विद्यालयः

Faculty

अध्यापकः: Sri Avadhanula Chinmaya Datta Ghanapathi

सहकारकः: Dr. Vamshi Krishna Ghanapathi

Course

  • Shaakala Samhita 64 Adhyaya (Divided into 8 Ashtakas/ 10 Mandalas)
  • Aitareya Brahmanam 40 Adhyayas (Divided into 8 Panchikas) & Aranyakam 5 Panchikas
  • Pada Paatha 64 Adhyaya; Krama Paatha 64 Adhyaya; Jataa Paatha 64 Adhyaya; Ghana Paatha 64 Adhyaya

Vedanga

  1. Shikshaa - Rigveda Praatishaakhyam
  2. Vyakaranam - Panineeyam
  3. Chhandas - Pingalam
  4. Niruktam - Yaaskam
  5. Jyotishyam - Lagadham
  6. Kalpa - Aashwalayana Shrouta Sutram, Gruhya Sutram

Syllabus

Year Grantha Courses Total
1 Shaakala Samhitaa Preparatory Courses including Swadharma 3 Adhyayas
2 Shaakala Samhitaa 1 Ashtaka 4-8 13 Adhyayas
3 Shaakala Samhitaa 2 Ashtaka 4-8 18 Adhyayas
4-14 Complete Samhita, Brahmanam, Vedanga studies continuing through advanced levels including Pada, Krama, Jata, and Ghana Paatha
