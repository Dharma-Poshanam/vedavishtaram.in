Faculty

अध्यापकः: Sri Dwibhashyam Hanumat Prasanna Ghanapathi

सहकारकः: Dr. Vamshi Krishna Ghanapathi

Course

  • Taittireeya Samhita 44 Prapaathaka (Divided into 7 Kaandas)
  • Taittireeya Brahmanam 28 Prapaathaka (Divided into 3 Ashtakas) & Aranyakam 12 Prapaathaka
  • Pada Paatha 44 Prapaathaka; Krama Paatha 44 Prapaathaka; Jataa Paatha 44 Prapaathaka; Ghana Paatha 44 Prapaathaka

Vedanga

  1. Shikshaa - Taittireeya Praatishaakhyam
  2. Vyakaranam - Panineeyam
  3. Chhandas - Pingalam
  4. Niruktam - Yaaskam
  5. Jyotishyam - Lagadham
  6. Kalpa - Aapastamba Shrouta Sutram, Aapastamba Gruhya Sutram, Aapastamba Shulba Sutra, Aapastamba Dharma Sutram

Syllabus

The 14-year comprehensive curriculum covers Taittireeya Samhita, Brahmanam, Aranyakam, all Vedangas, and advanced recitation methods including Pada, Krama, Jata, and Ghana Paatha.
