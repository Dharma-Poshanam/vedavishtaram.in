Vyakarana School व्याकरण विद्यालयः

Vyakarana is one of the six Vedangas, the science of Sanskrit grammar essential for proper understanding and interpretation of Vedic texts.

Course Overview

The Vyakarana school provides comprehensive training in Sanskrit grammar based on Panini's Ashtadhyayi and related classical texts.

Curriculum

  • Ashtadhyayi - Panini (8 Chapters, 4000 Sutras)
  • Mahabhashya - Patanjali
  • Vaiyakarana Siddhanta Kaumudi
  • Laghusiddhanta Kaumudi
  • Prakriya Texts
  • Paribhasha Sutras

Study Components

  • Sandhi (Euphonic Combinations)
  • Samasa (Compound Formation)
  • Pratyaya (Suffixes and Affixes)
  • Karaka (Case Relations)
  • Dhatu (Verbal Roots)
  • Subanta (Nominal Declensions)
  • Tinanta (Verbal Conjugations)
